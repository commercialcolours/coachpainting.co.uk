Coach painting in the UK

Whether you’re searching for a professional coach painting service to complete simple panel respray, dent or scratch repair or respraying, or even a full body overhaul, what should you look for?

  • Are all makes and models of coach catered for?
  • Do they should use the highest quality products?
  • Have they got the latest facilities?
  • Do their technicians have years of experience.
  • Is the company EPA registered or approved by the Office of Fair Trading.

Professional coach painting company

A coach company service should offer all types of paint finishes and use only high-quality products. They should match paint to manufacturers’ swatches using computerised paint mixing systems which produce exact colour matches to all commercial vehicles. Their environment should also be dust free which will provide a finish to match the most demanding standards.
